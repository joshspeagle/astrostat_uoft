Short Biography

Before becoming a graduate student in the Department of Astronomy at Harvard University, I was a researcher at the Kavli Institute for the Physics and Mathematics of the Universe (KIPMU) from 2015-2016, where I worked with Alexie Leauthaud and Kevin Bundy on improving photometric redshifts for the Hyper Suprime-Cam Subaru Strategic Program (HSC SSP) Survey. This work was partly funded by a CREST grant from the Japan Science and Technology Agency (JST).

Prior to my time at Kavli IPMU, I was an undergraduate student at Harvard University (2011-2015) where I completed my BA with Honors in Astrophysics and Physics.

Outside of work, I mostly enjoy hanging out at home with my wife and our dog.
