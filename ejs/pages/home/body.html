Welcome to the Astrostatistics Research Team (ART) website!

From the smallest stars to the largest galaxies, the ART is an interdisciplinary research group using cutting-edge statistics and artificial intelligence to unravel the mysteries of the Universe! Please use the links on the side to learn more about the exciting work that we do and the wonderful people who make it happen.

The ART is based out of the Department of Statistical Sciences and the David A. Dunlap Department of Astronomy & Astrophysics, with additional support from the Dunlap Institute for Astronomy & Astrophysics and the Data Sciences Institute.
